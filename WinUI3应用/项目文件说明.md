### Converters/DateTimeConverter.cs

### Converters/MessageBackgroundConverter.cs

## 配置文件说明

### appsettings.json

```json
{
  "ApiBaseUrl": "https://your-api-url.com",
  "SupabaseUrl": "https://your-project.supabase.co",
  "SupabaseKey": "your-supabase-key",
  "SignalRHubUrl": "https://your-api-url.com/hubs/chat"
}
```

**配置项说明**:

- **ApiBaseUrl**: 后端API的基础URL
- **SupabaseUrl**: Supabase项目的URL
- **SupabaseKey**: Supabase项目的匿名密钥
- **SignalRHubUrl**: SignalR Hub的URL
